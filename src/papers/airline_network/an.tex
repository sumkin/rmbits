  \frame{
    \frametitle{Network optimization.}
    NRPS does this.
    $$
    Rev = \langle f, x \rangle \rightarrow \max,
    $$
    $$
    A x \leq c,
    $$
    $$
    0 \leq x \leq \mu.
    $$
    where
    \begin{itemize}
      \item $\mu$ is vector of ODIFs demands.
      \item $A$ is product matrix.
      \item $c$ is vector of leg capacities.
      \item $f$ is vector of ODIF fares.
      \item $x$ is vector of allocations.
    \end{itemize}
  }
  
  \frame{
    \frametitle{Controlled vs Uncontrolled}
    Controlled:
    \begin{itemize}
      \item $f$ is our price (BMs).
      \item $A$ is about where we fly and SigOD list.
      \item $c$ is our capacity on leg.
    \end{itemize}
    Uncontrolled:
    \begin{itemize}
      \item $\mu$ is demand on ODIFs.
    \end{itemize}
